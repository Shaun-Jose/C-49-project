Panda Origami

Interesting Facts About Panda's

  • Giant pandas (often referred to as simply “pandas”) are black and white bears. In the wild, they are found in thick bamboo forests, high up in the mountains of central China
  • These magnificent mammals are omnivores. But whilst pandas will occasionally eat small animals and fish
  • Pandas are BIG eaters – every day they fill their tummies for up to 12 hours, shifting up to 12 kilograms of bamboo!

Pigeon Origami

Interesting Facts About Pegion's

  • Pigeons can adapt to a variety of settings, which means they are able to make changes that make it easier for them to live in a particular place.
  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals. They are one of only a small number of species to pass the ‘mirror test’
  • Pigeons are highly sociable animals. They will often be seen in flocks of 20-30 birds.
